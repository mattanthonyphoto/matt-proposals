About

About Copper Creek Homes

At Copper Creek Homes, we craft your dreams into reality. As the Lower Mainland’s premiere custom home builder and renovator, we bring the same passion to every one of our client’s projects — no matter how big or how small. From building a palatial custom home to renovating a walk-in closet, Copper Creek Homes can do it all.

Based in the heart of Abbotsford, BC, our leader, Marv Falk brings his toolbox, down-to-earth charm, and unmatched expertise to every building site. Your vision is the blue print, and we are here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you are looking to renovate your home or build brand new, Copper Creek Homes will help you achieve perfection with a personal touch.

We are proud to announce Copper Creek Homes was the winner of the 2024 CHBA Fraser Valley Housing Awards of Excellence for “Modern Farmouse, Whole House Under $250,000 - Renovation” !
