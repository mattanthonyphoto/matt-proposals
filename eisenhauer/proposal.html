Findings

Three findings from the data that matter most.

i

Munter owns the searches that should be yours

There's no location-specific service page, no dedicated "Kimberly custom home builder" page, no "East Kootenay luxury builder" page, no city-keyed content. iii

Your content velocity is zero

Your blog hasn't published in 18+ months. Your "In Progress" social series stopped 149 weeks ago. You have no LinkedIn presence at all. For a builder of your caliber, your digital footprint should be one of your biggest sales assets — right now it's a liability.

04 — The Plan
In priority order

Not everything is equally urgent. Here's the order, and why.

01 Fix the conversion Months 1–2

Before we drive more traffic, we stop wasting the traffic you already have.

  • Website rebuild on a 2026-standard structure. Location established in the first three seconds. Service-location pages for Kimberly, Cranbrook, Fernie, Invermere, Golden. GEO-ready structure for ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity citation. Interlinked pages. Page-speed optimized.
  • Image SEO done right. Every photo renamed with descriptive, search-optimized filenames. Alt text written for every image — critical for Google Images, accessibility, and generative search citation.
  • Portfolio overhaul. Four to five of your best finished and near-finished projects shot in one bundled Kootenay trip. Portfolio rebuilt around that imagery. Current imagery is good; new imagery will be remarkable.
  • Migration plan. I handle the Tamarack transition — domain retrieval, DNS cutover, analytics preservation, zero-downtime launch.
02 Beat Munter Months 2–6

Now we go after the rankings.

  • Keyword + GEO strategy. Target the 20 highest-intent terms in your market. Publish content that claims each one.
  • Two long-form blog posts per month, written in your voice using the brand-voice extraction system, optimized for both Google and generative search.
  • Monthly new page publishing — each month adds a new service page, location page, or pillar post. Twelve months from now, your site has 24+ new indexed pages targeting Munter's keyword set.
  • Rank tracking + monthly reporting. You see exactly where you rank against Munter week-over-week.
03 Build the distribution engine Months 3–6

Once the foundation holds, we turn on distribution.

  • LinkedIn launch. Company page, thought-leadership content for you, project features, behind-the-scenes. Your buyers are on LinkedIn. Your competitors aren't using it well. Open lane.

  • Instagram revival. "In Progress" series back on. Stories tied to active projects. Highlights properly curated. Feed pacing defined.
  • Local photographer brief (optional). I provide a pillar-mapped shot list. You source a Kootenay-based photographer for fill content between my shoot trips. Keeps the feed alive.

Your projects take 24 months. Your buyers decide over years, not weeks. A 12-month marketing engagement matches the pace of your actual business. A six-month engagement treats marketing like a project. It isn't.

On the 12-month tier, the full site rebuild is included. On every other tier, the website is a separate $5,000 build. That alone makes the 12-month tier the best per-dollar value by a wide margin (see the economics below).

Zero downtime. Zero lost authority.

  • Before anything else: one-page checklist to forward to Justina or Tamarack — domain ownership, DNS access, asset handoff, account logins.
