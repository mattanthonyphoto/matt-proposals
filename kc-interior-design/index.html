About the Studio

An all-female team of design professionals

Founded in 1996 by Kathy Renwick, KC Interior Design has built a reputation for creating inspiring spaces across the Fraser Valley. What began as a small business in Kathy's garage evolved into a respected design studio rooted in resilience, creativity, and community.

When Kathy retired in 2018, Lisa purchased KCID and continued the client-focused approach — a professional full-service firm that can do as much or as little as you need, from concept to completion.

What We Do

Full-service design,
from concept to completion

We can do as much or as little as you need — from a single room refresh to a complete new build.

Residential Design

New builds and renovations from the ground up. Finishes selection, lighting plans, furniture styling — designed around how you actually live.

Commercial Design

Offices, retail, and professional spaces that elevate your brand while supporting the people who work in them every day.

Developments & Assisted Living

Multi-unit developments and assisted living facilities. Scalable design that maintains quality, warmth, and consistency across every space.

Millwork & Wallpaper

Custom millwork design and installation, wallpaper sourcing and install. The details that transform a room from good to unforgettable.

3D Visualization

Photorealistic renderings and 3D visuals so you can see your space before a single material is ordered.

Drapery & Blinds

Custom drapery, blinds, and upholstery. The finishing layers that bring warmth, privacy, and personality to every room.
