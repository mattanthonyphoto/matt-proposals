Our Story

A Family Legacy
in Building

Founded by Mark Jauck in 2003, Marcraft Homes has grown from a single builder's vision into one of the Tri-Cities' most respected luxury home builders. Today, the Jauck family carries that vision forward together.

Matt Jauck, a BCIT Architectural Technology graduate, leads construction management with the precision and technical knowledge that complex builds demand. Andrea Jauck brings the client experience to life through sales, marketing, and her deep understanding of the communities we build in.

From Anmore's first net-zero luxury home to Gold Georgie Award-winning interiors, our work speaks to a simple belief: every home should be as extraordinary as the people who live in it.

Mark, Matt & Andrea Jauck

What We Build

Comprehensive
Building Services

01

Custom Homes

Ground-up luxury residences designed around your lifestyle, built with uncompromising attention to detail. From contemporary estates to fir post-and-beam timber homes.

02

Net-Zero Builds

Pioneering sustainable luxury with homes like TEN80 Uplands, Anmore's first net-zero residence. Solar integration, Tesla Powerwalls, and rainwater harvesting built into the design.

03

Renovations

Complete home transformations across Port Moody, Belcarra, and Anmore. Heritage restorations to modern overhauls, each treated with the same care as a new build.

04

Multi-Home Developments

Exclusive small-scale developments like Copper Beech Estates on Burke Mountain — seven luxury homes, sold out. Community-scale vision with custom-home quality.

05

Commercial

Commercial construction with the same craftsmanship standards we bring to residential. From tenant improvements to ground-up commercial builds.

06

Project Management

Full-service construction management with transparent budgets, regular updates, and a single point of accountability from permits to final walkthrough.
