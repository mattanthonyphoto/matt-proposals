Our Story

A Family
Legacy in Building

Founded by Mark Jauck in 2003, Marcraft Homes has grown from a single builder's vision into one of the Tri-Cities' most respected luxury home builders.

Matt Jauck, a BCIT Architectural Technology graduate, leads construction with the precision and technical depth that complex builds demand. Andrea Jauck brings the client experience to life through her deep understanding of the communities we build in.

From Anmore's first luxury net-zero home to Gold Georgie Award-winning interiors, our work speaks to one belief: every home should be as extraordinary as the people who live in it.

Mark, Matt & Andrea Jauck
I would like to express my heartfelt thanks for the stunning home you built for me. It turned out better than I ever imagined. Building a home can be an overwhelming undertaking, but Marcraft helped me through every step of the process.

Building Services

01

Custom Homes

Ground-up luxury residences designed for your lifestyle. Contemporary estates, post-and-beam timber homes, and everything in between.

02

Net-Zero Builds

Pioneering sustainable luxury. TEN80 Uplands, Anmore's first net-zero home, features 42 solar panels, Tesla Powerwalls, and rainwater harvesting.

03

Renovations

Complete home transformations across Port Moody, Belcarra, and Anmore. Heritage restorations to modern overhauls, treated with the same care as a new build.

04

Multi-Home Estates

Exclusive small-scale developments like Copper Beech Estates on Burke Mountain. Seven luxury homes, sold out. Community-scale vision, custom-home quality.

05

Commercial

Commercial construction held to the same craftsmanship standard we bring to our residential work. Tenant improvements to ground-up builds.

06

Project Management

Single-point accountability from permits to final walkthrough. Transparent budgets, weekly updates, and proactive problem-solving.
