Client Case Study

Building Balmoral's visual identity from the ground up

Photography. Website. SEO. Social. How one builder went from undocumented projects to a full creative system.

Navigate
The Client

Balmoral Construction

Custom home builder based in Whistler and Pemberton, BC. Led by Marc Harvey, Balmoral builds high-end residences across the Sea-to-Sky corridor, with projects ranging from contemporary alpine homes to mountain modern estates.

4 documented projects. Multiple design teams. One consistent visual standard.

The Challenge

Beautiful homes. No documentation.

Balmoral was building some of the most impressive custom homes in the corridor, but none of them were being documented professionally. No photography. No web presence beyond a basic Squarespace site. No SEO. No social media. Potential clients had no way to see the quality of their work.

Project Photography

Four projects. 95 images. Every angle documented.

Warbler, Fitzsimmons, Eagle, and Sugarloaf residences. Full-day shoots with drone coverage, interiors, exteriors, material details, and twilight photography across Whistler and Pemberton.

Website Rebuild

42 pages. Custom coded.

Complete Squarespace rebuild from a basic template site to a full marketing platform. 14 project pages with galleries, 6 service pages, 6 location pages targeting Sea-to-Sky keywords, and 7 blog posts. Every page hand-coded with structured data for search.

balmoralconstruction.com
SEO & Content Strategy

Found where it matters

6 location pages targeting "custom home builder Whistler," "renovation contractor Pemberton," and every variation in between. 7 blog posts covering cost guides, permits, and energy code compliance. Full JSON-LD structured data on every page.

Location Pages

6 geo-targeted pages for Whistler, Pemberton, and Squamish. Homes and renovations in each market.

Blog Content

7 SEO-driven posts answering real buyer questions. Cost guides, permit walkthroughs, project spotlights.

Structured Data

JSON-LD schema on every page. Project galleries, service definitions, local business markup.

Monthly Retainer

Ongoing SEO management at $892.50/mo. Monthly blog post, keyword monitoring, technical optimization.

Social Media

From zero to multi-channel

Built a complete content system for Balmoral across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Pinterest. Carousels, Reels concepts, project stories, and a Remotion-generated animated reel showcasing the partnership.

Instagram

8-week content calendar. Carousels, singles, stories with every caption written.

LinkedIn

14 posts queued. Project stories, partnership case study, thought leadership.

Pinterest

25 pins across all 4 projects. Rolling deployment through May.

Video

Animated reel built with Remotion. Beat-synced, spring physics, 5 layout types.

The Model

Not a vendor. A creative partner.

The Creative Partner retainer bundles photography, website management, SEO, and social media into a single monthly relationship. One point of contact. Every project documented. A visual system that grows with the business.

This is what it looks like when documentation becomes part of the build process, not an afterthought.
