Section 01

The Gap

Balmoral has been building luxury custom homes in the Sea to Sky corridor for 14 years, with zero awards submitted and zero press coverage.

That gap is costing real business. Competitors like RDC Fine Homes hold 17 Georgie Awards. CVC Custom Builders have been featured on HGTV and in the Wall Street Journal. In the luxury market, awards and publications are not vanity metrics. They are trust signals that close $2M+ contracts.

Prospective clients researching luxury builders compare credentials. The absence of awards is not neutral. It reads as unproven.

Every project Balmoral completes without submitting is a missed credential.

Section 03

Photography Requirements

All major awards share nearly identical photography specifications.

Submission Standards

  • 300 DPI JPEG, colour only
  • No people, logos, signage, or identifying features
  • No nighttime exteriors (dusk is acceptable)
  • Anonymous submissions (no branding visible)
  • Floor plans often required alongside images

This is exactly what my photography delivers. Every shoot is already award-ready.

Section 06

What gets done.
Month by month.

A 12-month awards calendar mapped to Balmoral's first submission cycle. Every deadline accounted for, every deliverable sequenced.

Q2 2026
Build the
Foundation
  • Join Sea to Sky CHBA to unlock Georgie and National eligibility
  • Photograph 2–3 strongest completed projects to award spec (300 DPI, anonymous, no branding)
  • Compile floor plans, project narratives, and builder statements for each
  • Pitch Warbler Residence to Western Living as exclusive feature
  • Submit Warbler to ArchDaily (free, no exclusivity conflict)
Q3 2026
Georgie Season
Opens
  • Georgie Awards entries open (June/July) — submit top 2 projects across multiple categories
  • CHBA National Awards entries open (September) — prepare submissions
  • Continue photographing active projects through peak summer light
  • Follow up on Western Living pitch; if accepted, coordinate feature timeline
  • Pitch Dwell and Architectural Digest with second project (exclusives)
Q4 2026
Submit
Everything
  • Georgie Awards deadline (November) — all entries finalized and submitted
  • HAVAN Awards entries open (October) — submit same projects, different categories
  • CHBA National deadline (December) — leverage Georgie entries for national submission
  • AZ Awards entries open (November) — submit strongest architectural project
  • Broader publication pitches: NUVO, Mountain Life, Azure Magazine
Q1 2027
Reap the
Credentials
  • Gold Nugget Awards submission (January deadline) — North American prestige
  • HAVAN deadline (January) — finalize entries
  • AZ Awards deadline (February) — submit
  • Dezeen Awards entries open (February) — global-tier submission
  • HAVAN Gala (April) — first potential award win
  • Georgie Gala (May) — BC's biggest night for builders
  • Use all awards, nominations, and press across website, proposals, and social
