Our Company

Custom craftsmanship in the heart of Whistler

Black Point Architectural Woodwork is a boutique millwork shop based in Function Junction, Whistler. Founded by Chris and Zane, both trade-certified Red Seal cabinetmakers with over 20 years of experience each, the shop specializes in bespoke millwork for residential and commercial spaces across the Sea to Sky Corridor.

Every project is a collaboration. We partner closely with architects, designers, and builders to bring each vision to life with precision, care, and an uncompromising standard of craftsmanship. From initial concept through final installation, our hands-on approach ensures every detail is executed beautifully.
