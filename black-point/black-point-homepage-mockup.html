The Shop

Two trade-certified cabinetmakers. 40 years combined.

Black Point was founded in 2017 by Chris McKenzie, an Australian-trained cabinetmaker with over 20 years in the trade. What started as a one-man workshop in Function Junction has grown into one of Whistler's most respected millwork studios.

Zane Brian joined in 2020, bringing another 20 years of fine cabinetmaking experience across three countries. He became a full partner in 2023. Together, they bring a shared standard: everything they build, they build to last.

"We couldn't be happier with the finished product. The attention to detail was second to none."

Ryan Pannell, Whistler Homeowner

Expertise

From concept to final install.

01

Custom Cabinetry

Kitchen, bathroom, and storage cabinetry designed and built to your architect's exact specifications. Every joint, every finish, considered.

02

Architectural Millwork

Feature walls, ceiling details, staircase elements, and bespoke timber features that define the character of a space.

03

Commercial Fit-Outs

Retail, hospitality, and office millwork built to withstand daily use without compromising design intent. See: Ecologyst, Whistler.

04

Built-In Solutions

Window seats, mudrooms, home offices, entertainment units. Functional joinery that becomes part of the architecture.

Our Process

From vision to handover.

Planning

Review drawings, refine details, and lock in materials with your design team.

Sourcing

Select timber, hardware, and finishes. Every material chosen for the project, not the shelf.

Fabricating

Hand-built in our Function Junction workshop. Precision CNC meets old-school joinery.

Installing

We install everything we build. No subcontractors, no surprises, no shortcuts.

Follow Up

Final walk-through, adjustments, and ongoing support. We stand behind every piece.
