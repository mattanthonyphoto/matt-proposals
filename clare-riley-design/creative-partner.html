Every single image alt attribute on your homepage is empty. All 27 of them. Google can't index what it can't read. What's built into this concept

Conversion design: Three CTAs placed at psychological decision points (hero, mid-portfolio, and contact). Award badge above the fold for instant credibility. "Limited projects per year" scarcity signal. A 4-step process section that reduces buyer anxiety by showing exactly how it works.

Brand voice: Every word was rewritten to match how Clare actually speaks — warm, collaborative, Australian-origin warmth with mountain context. Not generic design-studio copy.

User psychology: The page is sequenced as a decision journey: aspiration (hero) → trust (numbers bar) → proof (portfolio with interactive hover overlays) → clarity (process) → validation (testimonials) → action (contact). Each section answers the next question a visitor is thinking.
