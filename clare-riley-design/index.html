Founded in Pemberton, BC, Clare Riley Design is a full-service interior design studio. Originally from Australia, Clare brings a rare sense of warmth to mountain interiors, spaces that feel like they have a story before anyone walks in.

From ground-up custom builds to thoughtful renovations and mountain retreats, we work alongside architects and builders to design homes for how people actually want to live.

The Studio

Originally from Australia, Clare Riley brought her eye for light and texture to British Columbia's mountain landscape nearly two decades ago. Since founding the studio in 2017, her team has grown to four designers, each project grounded in the belief that a home should feel lived-in from the moment you step inside.

We work across the full spectrum of residential and commercial design: from ground-up custom builds to full renovations, vacation properties, and furniture sourcing. Every project begins with understanding how our clients want to live, then designing the space to make it effortless.
