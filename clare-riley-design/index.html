The Studio

Designed from here

Founded in 2017, Clare Riley Design is a full-service interior design studio based in Pemberton, British Columbia. Originally from Australia, Clare brings a rare sense of warmth to mountain interiors — drawing on a background in fashion merchandising and set design to create spaces that feel like they have a story before anyone walks in.

Our team of four works across Whistler, Pemberton, Squamish, and the Sea-to-Sky Corridor, designing homes that belong to their landscape.
