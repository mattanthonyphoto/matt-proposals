Our Approach

Designing for
how you live

At Clare Riley Design, we believe every mountain home should feel both beautiful and uniquely yours. Our studio brings a rare blend of creative vision and construction expertise to every project, whether it's a full custom build or a thoughtful renovation.

Based in Pemberton and deeply rooted in the Sea-to-Sky community, we understand the unique demands of mountain living. From hard-wearing materials that stand up to Whistler winters to layouts that frame the landscape, every detail is intentional.

We collaborate closely with your chosen builder and architect from day one, helping avoid costly changes later and creating spaces that work from the very first walkthrough.

What We Do

Design services for
every stage

01

Custom New Builds

Full-service interior design from blueprint to completion. We collaborate with your builder and architect from day one to create a cohesive, beautiful home.

02

Full Renovations

Thoughtful reconfigurations that honour the bones of your home while updating materials, layouts, and finishes for how you live today.

03

Vacation Properties

Guest-focused design for Whistler second homes and investment properties. Hard-wearing materials, turnkey furnishing, and rental-ready styling.

04

2D & 3D Renderings

Precision floor plans and photorealistic 3D visualizations that bring your space to life before construction begins, minimizing costly changes.

05

Furnishing & Styling

End-to-end sourcing, purchasing, delivery, and installation. We handle every detail so you arrive to a home that's move-in ready and truly yours.

06

Virtual Design

Remote design services for clients outside our immediate area. The same thoughtful process, delivered digitally with detailed specifications.

How We Work

From vision
to reality

Discovery

We learn how you live, what inspires you, and how your home should feel. Every great project starts with listening.

Concept & Planning

Space planning, material palettes, and 3D renderings give you a clear picture before any work begins.

Design Development

Detailed drawings, lighting plans, and specifications. We coordinate with your builder so nothing is left to chance.

Implementation

We manage procurement, trades coordination, and site visits. Every decision is made, so you don't have to.

Styling & Reveal

Furnishing, art, accessories, and the final details that transform a build into a home you never want to leave.

Featured Project

The Beer Farmers

Location Pemberton, BC
Scope Full Interior + Outdoor

A celebration of mountain hospitality in the heart of Pemberton. The outdoor dining room at The Beer Farmers was designed to bring the warmth of the interior experience outside, set against the dramatic backdrop of Mount Currie.

The space balances rustic charm with refined details, creating an atmosphere that feels both special and completely at home in its setting.
