Studio

We are a four-person design studio in Pemberton, BC, creating considered interiors for mountain homes across the Sea-to-Sky Corridor. Our work begins with listening and ends with spaces that feel as good as they look.

Every project balances warmth with restraint, choosing materials that honour the landscape and layouts that frame the light. We collaborate with builders and architects from the earliest stages, so nothing is an afterthought.
