What’s Missing (and What I’d Provide)

  • Zero video content. No project films, no reels, no build documentation footage. Video is the highest-engagement format on every platform in 2026, and Summerhill has none. This is the single biggest content gap.

  • 1,435 images missing alt text (87.6%). Google can’t index what it can’t read. I’d provide properly tagged, keyword-rich images with every delivery.
  • Social is sporadic. A 17-person company with active build sites should be posting 3-4 times a week. The content doesn’t exist yet to make that possible.
