What We Do

Painting Services

From fresh coats on new builds to full exterior transformations, we handle every type of painting project with the same attention to detail.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and cabinetry. We protect your floors and furniture, prep every surface properly, and leave your space looking brand new with clean, even coverage.

Exterior Painting

Full exterior prep, repair, and repaint. We handle siding, trim, decks, fences, and everything weather touches. Proper preparation means the finish holds up for years.

Commercial Painting

Offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and multi-unit buildings. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption and deliver a professional-grade finish on time.

New Construction

Working with builders from framing to final coat. We coordinate with your timeline, handle primer and finish coats, and deliver move-in ready results every time.

Staining & Refinishing

Deck staining, fence refinishing, and exterior wood care. We strip, sand, and apply quality stains that protect and enhance the natural grain of your wood.

Colour Consultation

Not sure where to start? We help you choose colours that complement your space, your lighting, and your style. The right palette makes all the difference.

The Colour Craft Process

How We Work

A clear, straightforward process from first call to final walkthrough. No surprises, no guesswork.

01

Free Estimate

We visit your property, assess the scope, and provide a detailed written estimate. No pressure, no obligation. You know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

02

Colour Selection

Need help choosing? We walk you through colour options that work with your space, lighting, and style. We can arrange samples so you see it before we start.

03

Prep & Paint

Proper prep is where quality starts. We clean, sand, patch, and prime before any paint goes on. Then we apply coats with precision, protecting everything around the work area.

04

Final Walkthrough

Before we pack up, we walk the project with you. Every edge, every corner, every detail. We clean up completely and don't leave until you're satisfied with the result.

About Us

The Colour Craft
Difference

Colour Craft Painting is a professional painting company serving residential and commercial clients across British Columbia. We built our reputation on thorough preparation, honest communication, and finishes that last.

Starting your project is straightforward. Request your free estimate online, by phone, or in person. We explain each step clearly, from colour selection to preparation and scheduling. With defined timelines and transparent pricing, you know what to expect from day one.

We're proud members of the Painting Contractors Association and hold full licensing and insurance. Every crew member is trained to our standards, and every project gets the same level of care, whether it's a single room or an entire commercial building.
