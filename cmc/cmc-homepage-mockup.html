About CMC Homes

Since its inception in 2010, Coastal Mountain Custom Homes has been building high-end homes and transformational remodels for the Whistler, Squamish, and Pemberton areas. Founded by Jamie Finlayson and Craig Glenday, two longtime Whistler locals, CMC Homes began small but quickly expanded into a tight-knit family with over 20 employees.

Over the years, CMC Homes has established itself as a trusted general contractor by architects, designers, and, most importantly, their clients. In everything they do, they cultivate strong relationships, thanks to their proactive, transparent, and communicative approach.

With 15 years spent building in the Sea to Sky region, CMC Homes continues to deliver luxury custom homes and high-end renovations.
