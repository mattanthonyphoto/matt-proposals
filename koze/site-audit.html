SEO Audit

Search visibility.
Solid start, specific gaps.

I crawled 9 pages on kozedesignbuild.ca. The site has a fast, clean Squarespace foundation with good basics in place, but two critical SEO elements are missing.

What’s Working
✓
Valid SSL + Fast Server
HTTPS active, 94ms response time. Clean, fast foundation.
✓
Strong Meta Title
Clear branded title in place. Google knows who you are.
✓
Open Graph Tags Present
Social sharing will display correctly with og:title and og:image.
✓
Sitemap Exists (8 URLs)
Google can discover your pages. Sitemap is properly configured.
✓
Single H1 Tag
Clean heading hierarchy with no duplicates.
What Needs Fixing
✗
No Meta Description
The homepage has no meta description. Google is pulling random text instead of a crafted description that sells the click. This is the single easiest SEO fix available.
✗
No Structured Data (Schema.org)
Zero JSON-LD markup. Google doesn’t know Koze is a builder, where you’re based, or what services you offer. Adding LocalBusiness schema would immediately improve local search presence.
✗
75% of Images Missing Alt Text
6 of 8 homepage images have no alt text. Across the full site, this means your project photography is invisible to Google Image search, which is a major discovery channel for renovation and design-build firms.

SEO Bottom Line

The foundation is solid (SSL, speed, sitemap, headings). Three targeted fixes would move the needle significantly: meta description (5 minutes), schema markup (30 minutes), and image alt text across all project photos. These are one-time fixes with permanent impact.

Local SEO & GEO Audit

Local visibility.
The biggest opportunity.

When someone searches “design build Pitt Meadows” or “custom renovation Maple Ridge,” can they find Koze? Right now, the local presence has gaps.

Google Business Profile
✗
No Google Business Profile Detected
Koze does not appear to have an active, optimized Google Business Profile. This is the single most important local SEO asset. Without it, you can’t appear in Google Maps or the Local Pack where 46% of local search clicks go.
✗
No LocalBusiness Schema
No structured data connecting your website to a local business identity. Google doesn’t know your address, service area, or business hours from the site alone.
Directory Presence
✓
Listed on Houzz
Active Houzz profile with project photos. Strong authoritative backlink for a design-build firm.
!
Not Found on HomeStars
HomeStars ranks well for “contractor” and “renovation” searches in the Lower Mainland. A profile would add another citation and trust signal.
!
Not Found on CHBA Directory
No CHBA Fraser Valley listing detected. This is an authoritative industry backlink and required for housing award eligibility.

Local SEO Bottom Line

Google Business Profile is the #1 priority. Free, 30 minutes to set up. Add your Pitt Meadows address, service areas (Maple Ridge, Coquitlam, Port Moody, Fraser Valley), project photos, and request 5-10 Google reviews from past clients. This single action changes local visibility within 60 days.
