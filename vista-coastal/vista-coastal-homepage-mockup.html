About Vista Coastal Builders

How we started and why we build fine homes

The story of Vista Coastal Builders began with Brian Pollock. Brian’s story began with building throughout Western Canada since the 1970’s.  Brian worked on a multitude of projects throughout the years, ranging from institutional, commercial and his personal passion, high-end custom homes.

In 1995, while working as a Site Supervisor on the Furry Creek Golf & Country Club Clubhouse, Brian developed a relationship with world-renowned architect Brian Hemingway. This marked the beginning of a relationship that has lasted over 30 years and continues to this day.
