Future shoots, horizontal and vertical.

Per your note, every shoot from here forward is captured for both desktop and mobile. Below is how I'll be doing it, what's included, and what it costs.

Video · shoot wide, dual-crop in post
Every video setup is framed for both 16:9 and 9:16 in the same take. Trades won't repeat for a second pass, and the camera has enough headroom that the vertical cut doesn't lose quality. You get a horizontal master and a vertical cut from the same coverage.
Stills · double takes on hero scenes
Composition is the entire point of a still, so hero scenes get captured twice. Once horizontal for desktop banners and proposals, once vertical or 4:5 for mobile and social. Walk-around documentation stays single-format.
Voice · mic'd interview b-roll
Lavalier mics on you, the trades, and the homeowner where consent allows. Captured as ambient, not staged talking heads. Short real-voice clips you can drop into reels, sales calls, and lead nurture.
