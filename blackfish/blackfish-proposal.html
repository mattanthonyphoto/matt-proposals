Website Assessment

Your site is built on a custom PHP framework that loads content via JavaScript. When I ran it through extraction tools — the same tools search engines use — I got back nothing but CSS. No text. No headings. No content. Google likely can't read your site either.

All page titles are identical: "High Performance Custom Home Builder & Renovator - [PAGE]". No location keywords. No differentiation. No meta descriptions.

The homepage opens with "RECENT WORK" — no hero, no value proposition, no clear call to action. There are no project case studies. No testimonials on-site. No team page. No awards showcase. One news article from 2019.

The tech stack is aging: Bootstrap Glyphicons, Font Awesome 4.5, Slick Carousel — mid-2010s tools that are now 10+ years old.

What This Means

When a prospect searches "custom home builder North Vancouver," Blackfish likely doesn't appear. Your Houzz profile, NV Chamber listing, and directory pages are doing your SEO work for you. Your own website is essentially invisible to organic search.
