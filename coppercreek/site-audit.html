SEO Audit

Strong content.
Two technical gaps holding it back.

I crawled 30 pages on coppercreekhomes.ca. The content foundation is excellent, 22 project pages with descriptions, a blog, and 1,336 images. But two specific technical issues are limiting search visibility.

What’s Working
✓
Fast, Secure Foundation
Valid SSL, 84ms response time, clean Squarespace build.
✓
76-URL Sitemap
Extensive sitemap helping Google discover all pages. Well above average.
✓
Open Graph Tags Present
Social sharing displays correctly.
✓
Strong Content Depth (100/100)
22 project pages, all with descriptions. 645-word homepage. Blog with 5 posts. This is significantly better than most Fraser Valley builders.
✓
Excellent Visual Coverage (92/100)
1,336 images across the site. Project pages average 53.8 images each. Only 33 images missing alt text (2.5%). Outstanding.
What Needs Fixing
✗
No Structured Data (Schema.org)
Zero JSON-LD markup on the site. Google doesn’t know Copper Creek is a builder, where you’re based, or what services you offer. Adding LocalBusiness + HomeBuilder schema would improve local search results and could unlock rich snippets.
!
Meta Title Too Short (18 Characters)
"Copper Creek Homes" is the full title. Should include location and service: "Copper Creek Homes | Custom Builder & Renovator | Abbotsford BC" for better click-through rates.
!
Meta Description Too Long (228 Characters)
Google truncates at ~155 characters. The extra text is invisible in search results. Tighten to hit within 150-155 characters for full visibility.

SEO Bottom Line

The content and visual foundation is among the best I’ve audited in the Fraser Valley. The only real blocker is structured data, a one-time fix that would make Google understand Copper Creek as a local builder. The meta title and description adjustments are 5-minute fixes with immediate impact.

Local SEO & GEO Audit

Local visibility.
The one big gap.

When someone in Abbotsford or the Fraser Valley searches for a custom builder, can they find Copper Creek on Google Maps?

Google Business Profile
✗
No Google Business Profile Detected
This is the single biggest gap in Copper Creek’s online presence. Without an active GBP, you can’t appear in Google Maps or the Local Pack (where 46% of local search clicks go). For a builder that serves 18 specific areas, this is a significant missed opportunity.
✗
No LocalBusiness Schema
No structured data connecting the website to a local business identity. Google can’t programmatically understand your address, service areas, or business hours.
Directory Presence
✓
Listed on Houzz
Active Houzz profile. Strong authoritative backlink.
✓
Listed on BBB
Better Business Bureau profile found. Good trust signal.
✓
Listed on TrustedPros
Two TrustedPros listings found. Additional citation source.
!
Not Found on HomeStars
HomeStars ranks well for contractor searches in the Fraser Valley. Worth creating a profile.
!
CHBA Fraser Valley Membership
You won a CHBA FV award but the CHBA directory listing wasn’t detected in the audit. Verify your member profile is active and linked to the website.

Local SEO Bottom Line

Set up a Google Business Profile. It’s free, takes 30 minutes, and with your CHBA award, 22 project photos to upload, and 18 service areas to list, it would be one of the strongest GBP profiles in the Fraser Valley builder category. This is the highest-ROI action available.
