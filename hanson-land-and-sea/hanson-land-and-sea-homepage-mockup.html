About Hanson Land and Sea

Custom Home Builders on the Sunshine Coast, BC – Crafting Bold and Timeless West Coast Architecture

Hanson Land & Sea is a family-owned company deeply rooted in the Sunshine Coast community. We specialize in custom homes, major renovations, and thoughtfully designed additions that embrace the natural beauty of the West Coast. Whether designing in-house or collaborating with your architect, we’re dedicated to bringing your vision to life with craftsmanship, creativity, and care.

As licensed contractors, we work alongside a trusted network of skilled sub trades to deliver exceptional results throughout the Sunshine Coast and surrounding islands. From ground-up builds to transformative renovations, our team is committed to sustainable construction practices that stand the test of time.
