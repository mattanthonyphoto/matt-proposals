About Ario

Mountain Building
Since 2007

Founded by Chris Addario in 2007, Ario Construction has spent nearly two decades building custom homes in one of the most demanding environments in British Columbia.

As a Certified Passive House Consultant and Tradesperson, Chris brings a rare combination of energy expertise and hands-on construction knowledge to every project. He serves on the CHBA Sea to Sky board and is a former president of the Sea to Sky Home Builders Association.

From our workshop on Alpha Lake Road, we build homes and commercial spaces that respect the mountain landscape and perform for the people who live and work in them.
