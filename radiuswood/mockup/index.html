About Radius

The shop the hardest projects get sent to.

Founded in Burnaby in 2000 by Rick Wilson, BCIT-trained Master Joiner and founder. Twenty craftspeople, half European-trained. Five thousand square feet of bench space. Traditional joinery throughout, no CNC by choice.

The shop has built residential and estate millwork across British Columbia for two and a half decades. AWMAC Gold winner in 2011. Five-time Best of Houzz between 2013 and 2017.

How We Work

Six steps from drawing to install.

The same process for a single coffee table or eighteen months of integrated estate work. What changes is how many full-scale mock-ups we build before we cut.

01

Design

From your drawing set or developed in-house. CAD shop drawings, 3D renders where they help.

02

Planning

Lead times, species sourcing, mock-up scheduling. The schedule we ship from.

03

Production

Twenty craftspeople. Solid stock and veneer-laid work. Traditional joinery. Zero CNC.

04

Mock-up

On novel joinery, a full-scale piece comes first. The client sees it before we commit.

05

Finishing

Up to six coats of conversion varnish, between-coat sanded. Finish schedules to two pages where they need to be.

06

Installation

In-house installers. Site-measure, template, scribe-fit, punch list.
