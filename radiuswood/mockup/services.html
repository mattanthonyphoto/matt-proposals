Services

Architectural millwork. Cabinetry. Furniture.

What We Build

Six categories. One bench. Same standard.

We work residential and estate across British Columbia. Solid stock and veneer-laid. Traditional joinery throughout. The same six-step process from a single coffee table to eighteen months of integrated millwork.

01

Architectural Millwork

Wall paneling, coffered ceilings, slat walls, integrated case goods. The work that runs continuously across rooms and asks the most of figure-matching and reveals.

  • Slip-matched + book-matched veneer panels
  • Coffered + flat-panel ceilings
  • Slat walls with integrated pulls
  • Built-ins, libraries, full-room cladding
02

Custom Kitchens

Full kitchens from drawing set to install day. Solid stock and veneer-laid. Conversion-varnish or hand-rubbed-oil finishes. The shop's highest-volume category.

  • Solid-wood frame and panel
  • Slab-front modern with clean reveals
  • Touch-latch + flush-pull hardware
  • Furniture-grade interiors
03

Wine Cellars

Climate-controlled cellars in cedar, quarter-sawn oak, or species to spec. Templated on site over multiple visits before the first piece comes out of the shop.

  • Cedar + quarter-sawn racks
  • Climate-sealed glass surrounds
  • Integrated stone and millwork transitions
  • Single-bottle and case-rack mixes
04

Bars + Built-Ins

Home bars, libraries, office built-ins. Often with laminated curves and matched-veneer back walls. Detail-heavy work where the joinery has to do the talking.

  • Bent lamination + curved millwork
  • Back-bar mirror + lighting integration
  • Library shelving systems
  • Office case goods, book-matched
05

Custom Furniture

Dining tables, cabinets, single-piece furniture. Walnut, oak, maple, anigre, and figured species. The pieces clients commission once and keep.

  • Solid-stock dining tables
  • Single-piece cabinetry
  • Coffee tables with inlay work
  • Heirloom-grade joinery
06

Project Consultation

Pre-design value engineering, mockup work, finish-schedule development. The conversations that happen before a project becomes a quote.

  • Shop visits + mockup walk-throughs
  • Material sourcing + specification
  • Joinery and structural review
  • Finish samples + color development
How We Work

Six steps from drawing to install.

The same process for a single coffee table or eighteen months of integrated estate work. What changes is how many full-scale mock-ups we build before we cut.

01

Design

From your drawing set or developed in-house. CAD shop drawings, 3D renders where they help.

02

Planning

Lead times, species sourcing, mock-up scheduling. The schedule we ship from.

03

Production

Twenty craftspeople. Solid stock and veneer-laid. Traditional joinery. Zero CNC.

04

Mock-up

On novel joinery, a full-scale piece comes first. The client sees it before we commit.

05

Finishing

Up to six coats of conversion varnish, between-coat sanded.

06

Installation

In-house installers. Site-measure, template, scribe-fit, punch list.
