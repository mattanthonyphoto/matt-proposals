Services

Custom millwork, concept to install.

Overview

Services for the luxury estate market & abroad.

Radius offers services in custom architectural millwork, cabinetry, and furniture to both the British Columbia market and abroad. We primarily complete millwork and cabinet packages for some of the most luxurious estate homes — and custom furniture built to suit any decor.

Every project goes through a rigorous, six-phase planning and execution process before production begins. Traditional and contemporary joinery is engineered into all fabrication. Furniture-grade finishes through our in-house finishing department. Every step under one roof, every detail managed by the team that builds it.

  • 01

    Architectural Millwork

    Custom cabinetry, wall panelling, case goods, passage and entry doors. Slip-matched and book-matched veneer, hand-cut dovetails, mortise-and-tenon joinery. The core service that runs the shop — from kitchens and baths through libraries, wine rooms, and master suites.

    View →
  • 02

    Custom Furniture

    Quality custom furniture for the home or office, built to suit any decor. Traditional and contemporary joinery engineered into every piece, finished in-house. Standalone pieces or paired with a millwork package.

    View →
  • 03

    Wine Cellars

    Walnut, rift white oak, and bookmatched anigre wine rooms. Display racking, pull-out cellars, integrated lighting, climate coordination. Capacities from a few hundred bottles to multi-thousand-bottle collections, glass-fronted or fully enclosed.

    View →
  • 04

    Bent Lamination

    Twelve layers of veneer over a curved form. Custom jigs per radius, spring-back logged per humidity. The AWMAC Gold service — curved rotundas, structural radii, two-storey curved foyers up to twenty-foot runs.

  • 05

    In-House Finishing

    Conversion varnish, hard-wax oil, between-coat sanded. Antique cottage to high-gloss contemporary — every finish under our own roof, never sub-contracted. Access to an exceptional supply chain of custom suppliers so design intent translates accurately.

    3D Renderings

    Industry-specific CAD with texture. Precisely scaled measurements result in an accurate, real-life digital depiction of your project. Review every detail before production begins; minimize delays and costly errors on the floor.

The Process

Six phases through the shop.

Every project moves through a rigorous, integrated process from drawing review to install. Same team from start to finish. Industry-specific CAD software, full mock-ups of every project, in-house finishing, in-house install. Quality checks, site visits, punch lists — all closed by the team that built it.

01

Design

Quotations from existing drawings or original concepts. CAD with 3D rendering and texture. Design consultation.

02

Planning

Engineering with CAD shop drawings. Site conditions verified. Sub-trade integration locked in early.

03

Production

Journeymen and master joiners work in unison on the production floor. Slip-matched veneers from the same flitch.

04

Mock-Up

Unique to Radius. A complete mock-up of the project at the shop — fit, finish, appliances, design — evaluated before final commit.

05

Finishing

Thorough preparation for stain, lacquer, or paint. Highest-quality tooling and materials. Conversion varnish, six coats.

06

Install

Shipped and installed by in-house staff. Quality checks, site visits, every punch-list item closed by the team that built it.
