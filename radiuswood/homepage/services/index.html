Services

Custom millwork, concept to install.

What we do

Six service categories, under one roof.

Every project is bespoke. Architectural cabinetry for kitchens, baths, libraries, and offices. Wall panelling through public rooms — slip-matched walnut, rift oak, bookmatched anigre. Custom furniture with traditional and contemporary joinery engineered in. Wine cellars with vertical-rod racking and integrated lighting. Bent lamination for rotundas and structural radii up to twenty-foot runs (the AWMAC Gold service). In-house finishing from antique cottage to high-gloss contemporary, never sub-contracted out.
