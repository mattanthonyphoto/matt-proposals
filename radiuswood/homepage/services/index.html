Architectural cabinetry, panelling, wine cellars, bent-laminated curved work, and furniture. Twenty craftspeople, one bench, twenty-five years on Government Street.

What we do

Six service categories, under one roof.

Every project is bespoke. We work from the architect's drawing set, or develop the millwork package alongside the design team. Drafting, mock-up, production, finishing, install, punch-list — all in-house, all by the same team.

The list below names the core services. In practice they blur — a Vancouverhouse penthouse project touches cabinetry, panelling, bent lamination, and furniture across the same scope. Send us the set and we'll come back with a feasibility read.

  • 01

    Architectural Millwork

    Custom cabinetry, wall panelling, case goods, passage and entry doors. Slip-matched and book-matched veneer, hand-cut dovetails, mortise-and-tenon joinery. The core service that runs the shop.

    View →
  • 02

    Custom Furniture

    Quality custom furniture for the home or office. Traditional and contemporary joinery engineered into every piece, finished in-house. Standalone pieces or paired with a millwork package.

    View →
  • 03

    Wine Cellars

    Walnut, rift white oak, and bookmatched anigre wine rooms. Display racking, pull-out cellars, integrated lighting, climate coordination. Capacities from 200 bottles to 5,000+.

    View →
  • 04

    Bent Lamination

    Twelve layers of veneer over a curved form. Custom jigs per radius, spring-back logged per humidity. The AWMAC Gold service. Rotundas, curved foyers, structural radii up to twenty-foot runs.

    View →
  • 05

    In-House Finishing

    Conversion varnish, hard-wax oil, between-coat sanded. Antique cottage to high-gloss contemporary — all finishes done under our own roof, never sub-contracted out.

    View →
  • 06

    3D Renderings

    Industry-specific CAD with texture, full mock-ups of every project at the shop. Lets you review every detail before production begins — and avoid costly errors on the floor.

    View →
