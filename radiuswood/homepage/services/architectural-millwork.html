Services · Architectural Millwork

The core service. Built at the bench.

Overview

For some of the most luxurious estate homes in British Columbia & abroad.

Radius is proud to offer services in custom architectural millwork, cabinetry, and furniture to both the British Columbia market and abroad. We primarily complete millwork and cabinet packages for some of the most luxurious estate homes — and custom furniture built to suit any decor.

The scope can be a single feature wall or the millwork package for a whole house. Indian River, Vancouverhouse, Waterfront, and Lion's Bay were all full-house scopes. Whatever the project, the standard is the same: slip-matched veneer, hand-cut joinery, conversion-varnish finish, in-house install.

The Process

Six phases, one team.

Every project moves through the same rigorous, integrated process from drawing review to install. Same hands from start to finish. We measure twice, we test the joint, we don't ship a piece until it's right.

01

Design + Quotation

We provide quotations from existing drawings or develop concepts ourselves. Industry-specific CAD software with 3D rendering and texture, plus specialized design consultation as the package takes shape.

02

Planning + Engineering

Once the design and budget are approved the project moves into engineering. Projects are engineered for building with CAD shop drawings, and site conditions are verified. Sub-trade coordination locked in early.

03

Production

Journeymen and master joiners work in unison on the production floor. Slip-matched veneers sequenced from the same flitch. Hand-cut dovetails, mortise-and-tenon, bent lamination over custom jigs — joinery engineered into every piece.

04

Mock-Up

Unique to the Radius process. A complete mock-up of the project is created in the shop, allowing clients to evaluate fit, finish, integration of appliances, and design before the project is finalized for shipping.

05

Finishing

Thorough preparation for stain, lacquer, or paint, using the highest-quality tooling and materials. Conversion varnish, six coats, between-coat sanded — all done under our own roof, never sub-contracted out.

06

Installation

Projects are shipped and installed by in-house staff. Quality verification checks are performed from drawing review through final punch list — every site visit and punch-list item closed by the team that built it.
