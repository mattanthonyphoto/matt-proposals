Services · 3D Renderings

See it built, before we cut.

Home/Services/3D Renderings

Industry-specific CAD with texture. Full-scale mock-ups at the shop. Review every detail in real proportions before production begins.

Why

Catch the issue on the screen, not on the wall.

3D renderings are how we de-risk a project. Every render incorporates precisely scaled measurements that result in an accurate, real-life digital depiction of your project. We catch dimensional issues, slip-match transitions, and sub-trade collisions before we cut a single piece of stock.

Created from photos, drawings, designs, or charts. Allows planners to view a realistic example of a design and construction site. Helps avoid construction delays and minimize costly errors.

  • Residential. Full-house millwork packages, single feature walls, custom furniture.
  • Commercial. Office, hospitality, retail millwork — concept through to construction documents.
  • Interior. Cabinetry, panelling, ceilings, integrated lighting, joinery details.
  • Exterior. Cladding details, integrated cedar systems, custom doors and gate work.
Process

Render, mock, refine, cut.

Renders are step two of the build — between concept drawings and the bench. We render every project, every time. Then we mock the critical assemblies in the shop so you can see the millwork at scale before we commit to the final stock.

Visit the shop and walk through the project — the mock-ups, the material samples, the joinery details. Friday afternoons usually work best.
