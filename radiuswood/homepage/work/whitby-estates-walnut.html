Whitby Estates Walnut

The Project

Cherry-walnut panelling with hand-carved corbels.

The Maghsoud Residence at Whitby Estates — a traditional Persian-influenced estate where the millwork vocabulary is cherry-walnut panelling, coffered ceilings, and hand-carved corbels over every mantel and counter overhang. The whole-house package: media room, library, butler's pantry, his-and-hers walk-ins, a foyer rotunda, a wrought-iron stair, and an elevator cab lined in matching cherry. The mahogany double doors at the entry carry crotch-mahogany inlay panels — the figured-grain section of the log, hand-cut and book-matched into the stile-and-rail frame. Hand-carved corbels are turned and chiseled at the bench, then finished to match the cherry. The crown mouldings are dentil-pattern. Every detail finished by hand.
