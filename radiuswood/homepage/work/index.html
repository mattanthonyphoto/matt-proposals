Selected Work

Eight projects from the last decade on the bench.

Selected residential estate work across Vancouver, the North Shore, the Sea-to-Sky, and Whistler. Whole-house millwork packages, gallery wine bars, bent-laminated rotundas, diamond-bookmatched ceilings, slip-matched rift oak through public rooms. Featured projects below: Vancouverhouse BOP Penthouse (inside BIG's Vancouver House), Indian Arm Study (cedar-lined waterfront cabin), Indian River — The Point (slip-matched rift oak gable-end residence), West Vancouver Modern Luxury (banded cedar-slat ceiling), Waterfront Anigre Penthouse (diamond-bookmatched ceiling), Lion's Bay Residence (oceanfront painted millwork), Contemporary Walnut Estate, and Whitby Estates Walnut. AWMAC BC Gold winner Rosebery Estate also viewable.
