Waterfront Anigre Penthouse

The Project

Diamond-bookmatched anigre over a gallery wine bar.

A harbor-front penthouse where the ceiling does the heavy lifting. Diamond-bookmatched anigre panels over a steel armature, sequenced so that every diamond rotates the flitch ninety degrees — the figure reads as one woven plane from any vantage. Beneath it: a Bocci pendant cluster, a granite-topped kitchen island, and a gallery wine bar built around the owner's collection of Inuit soapstone sculpture. Lit anigre display niches step across the back wall behind tempered glass; the bottle racks pull out as a single carriage. An art-deco stair at the entry — travertine treads, LED-lit risers, open anigre shelving wall on the inside line — reads as a private museum the moment you step in. Photography by Hempenstall.
