Lion's Bay Residence

The Project

Antique-white painted millwork on Howe Sound.

A traditional oceanfront residence on Howe Sound with a view of the islands you don't have to be told to look at. Antique-white painted millwork throughout — the cabinetry, the panelling, the corbel-carrying mantels, the beadboard inserts. A red Viking range as the kitchen's single saturated note. The architectural move is a curved dining nook that wraps the corner of the public room, framing the harbour through a bay window. Cabinetry is hard maple, painted at the shop and finished with a low-VOC conversion varnish that holds up to coastal humidity. The glaze is hand-wiped in a single application — the figure follows the carved profiles into the corners where painters don't always go. Hand-carved corbels sit under every mantel.
