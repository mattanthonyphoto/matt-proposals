West Vancouver Modern Luxury

The Project

Banded cedar-slat ceilings over an ocean-view coffee bar.

A modern coastal residence with the kind of view the architecture is built to frame. Cedar-slat ceiling bands with integrated downlight strips run across the main public rooms — a ceiling that's also the lighting plan. Each band runs the full length of the room; the joint where the band breaks is hidden in shadow. Glass curtain walls slide open to a cantilevered ocean-view deck. The kitchen and coffee bar share the same vocabulary: saffron-painted upper cabinets, grey textured base cabinets, AEG appliances. The interior's counter-note is a pair of antique carved double doors sourced from an estate sale and re-hung as the entry to a wine cellar lined with spiral racking — patinated old wood against the clean modern shell.
