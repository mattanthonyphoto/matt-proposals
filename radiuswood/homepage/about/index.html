Who We Are

An elite level of craftsmanship, continuously evolving.

Radius Architectural Millwork Ltd. is a dynamic team of cabinetmakers, finishers, and installers providing an elite level of craftsmanship and service to British Columbia's most exclusive estates. The attention to detail we put into every project is uncommon — even within the luxury millwork marketplace.

Every project goes through a rigorous planning process before production begins, ensuring seamless integration with sub-trades. Traditional and contemporary joinery is engineered into all fabrication — custom mouldings, profiles, doors, casework. A wide variety of furniture-grade finishes through our in-house finishing department, from antique country cottage to high-gloss contemporary.

With access to an exceptional supply chain of custom suppliers, we obtain the range of materials needed to accurately translate any design intent. We pride ourselves on creating unique finishes that distinguish our work from the competition.
