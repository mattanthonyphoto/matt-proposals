About

Twenty-five years on the same bench.

The Shop

A Master Joiner-led custom millwork shop in Burnaby.

Founded by Rick Wilson in 2002 — BCIT-trained Master Joiner with a decade at Wilms before he opened the bench on Government Street. Today the floor runs twenty craftspeople: cabinetmakers, finishers, installers, all employees, all in-house. AWMAC BC Gold in 2011 with Lamoureux Architects. Five consecutive years of Best of Houzz. Selected by Shift Interiors. Featured inside the Bjarke Ingels Vancouver House. Same shop, same bench, same address since the day we opened.
