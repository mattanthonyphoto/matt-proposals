Overview

The shop, in plain terms.

Radius Architectural Millwork is a custom shop in Burnaby, BC. Founded in 2002 by Rick Wilson, BCIT-trained Master Joiner. Twenty craftspeople on the floor — cabinetmakers, finishers, installers — all employees, all in-house.

We build architectural cabinetry, panelling, wine cellars, curved bent-laminated work, and furniture for residential estate projects across British Columbia. AWMAC BC Gold 2011 with Lamoureux Architects. Five consecutive years Best of Houzz, 2013–2017. Same address since the day we opened.
