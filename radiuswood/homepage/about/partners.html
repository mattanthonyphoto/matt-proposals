How we work

From the architect's drawing set to the punch-list.

Most of our projects come in through architects and interior designers we've worked with before. We slot into the project at the drawing-review stage — early enough that we can flag dimensional issues with the millwork run, late enough that the design intent is locked. Then we develop the shop drawings, mock the critical assemblies, and run the project bench-to-install.

The list below is selected — not exhaustive. We name the firms whose work we're allowed to credit publicly, and the photographers whose images live on this site.
