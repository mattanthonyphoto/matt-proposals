Architects, designers, photographers.

How we work

From the architect's drawing set to the punch-list.

Most projects come in through architects and interior designers we've worked with before. We slot in at the drawing-review stage — early enough to flag dimensional issues in the millwork run, late enough that the design intent is locked. Selected collaborations include Lamoureux Architects (AWMAC BC Gold 2011 — Rosebery Estate), Shift Interiors (Indian River — The Point), and a penthouse inside Bjarke Ingels Group's Vancouver House. Photography across the portfolio by Andrew Latreille (LAP), Kent Leckie, Hempenstall, and Conrad Brown.

Working on a project?

We'd like to see the set. Send drawings.

Whether you're the architect, the designer, or the homeowner — we respond within two business days with a feasibility read and a tested quote.
