Twenty craftspeople, five thousand square feet, twenty-five years on Government Street — and a Master Joiner on the floor every day of it.

Origin

Rick started Radius in 2002.

BCIT for joinery. A decade at Wilms Millwork on the bench. The Government Street shop opened in 2002 with three people and a long-radius bending form for a single curved alder rotunda. The curve held. The shop grew around the bench.

Today the floor runs twenty craftspeople — journeymen, finishers, installers — all employees, all in-house. Concept to completion under one roof, twenty-five years on the same address.

Timeline

What the shop has done since the doors opened.

2002
Radius opens on Government Street in Burnaby. Three people, one bending press, a contract for a curved alder rotunda.
2011
AWMAC BC Gold — Best in Quality + Best in Service. Rosebery Estate with Lamoureux Architects. $1.5M millwork scope. Eighteen months on the bench, fifteen craftspeople full-time.
2013–2017
Best of Houzz — five consecutive years. Design + Service categories. Influencer status across the platform with 10,000+ saves.
2018
Waterfront Anigre Penthouse — diamond-bookmatched anigre ceiling, gallery wine bar with Inuit soapstone collection. Photography by Hempenstall.
2020
Vancouverhouse BOP Penthouse — full-house millwork inside Bjarke Ingels Group's twisting Vancouver House tower. Photography by Kent Leckie.
2025
Indian River — The Point. Slip-matched rift white oak interior, river-rock chimney column, tongue-and-groove stair. Photography by Andrew Latreille.
2027
AWMAC Submission — Old Gravel Road, Whistler. Forty-three-sheet rift-and-smoked-oak package, currently on the bench.
Today

The people on the floor.

Rick Wilson · Owner + Master Joiner. BCIT trained. Twenty-five years at the bench. Every project on the floor passes through Rick at the drawing review and again at the install.

Twenty craftspeople · Cabinetmakers, finishers, installers. Journeymen and apprentices. All employees, all in-house. No sub-contracted finishing, no sub-contracted install.

Two design tables · Drafting, mock-ups, sub-trade coordination. Industry-specific CAD, 3D renderings, full mock-ups of every major millwork run before we cut the final stock.
