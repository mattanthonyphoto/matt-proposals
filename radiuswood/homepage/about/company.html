The Shop

A premier manufacturer of custom cabinetry & millwork.

Radius is a high-end manufacturer of custom cabinetry and millwork for new home construction and renovations. Established in 2000 — by President & Founder Rick Wilson — we have spent over two decades building a reputation as a premier shop in the custom cabinetry industry.

We take great pride in the skill level of our staff and their capabilities as superior craftsmen. From planning and cost-estimates through manufacturing, finishing, and installation, we continually research and develop better ways to surpass our clients' expectations. We pursue perfection by always using the best-quality hardwoods, sheet goods, hardware, and finishes to produce a product that is a cut above.

Letter from the President

"Radius pursues perfection by always using the best quality hardwoods, sheet goods, hardware and finishes to produce a product that is clearly a cut above."

We work directly with clients to achieve the vision of any new home project requiring a high level of fit and finish. We excel at problem-solving techniques and integrating seamlessly with sub-trades and contractors to ensure jobs go smoothly. Meeting schedules, quality standards, and budgetary targets is always at the forefront of our considerations. We understand the construction industry and strive to meet or exceed industry standards.

As an AWMAC Gold award winning shop, we have a proven track record of providing a level of quality, service, and craftsmanship that is a cut above the industry standards.

Clients can expect a skilled, professional, and high-quality job from our team at Radius. We bring a level of confidence to our clients in their decision to use Radius as their millwork manufacturer of choice — by ensuring that the past experiences of our clients is always repeated. In any construction project, efficiency is born from good communication and integration. Our ability to integrate seamlessly with sub-trades and contractors brings a level of efficiency that can save our clients a lot of time, money, and stress.

We look forward to the opportunity to establish new long-term relationships with clients who truly understand the value in quality craftsmanship and professionalism.

Rick WilsonPresident & Founder
Timeline

What the shop has done since the doors opened.

Twenty-five years of custom cabinetry and millwork across British Columbia and abroad — for new home construction and renovations, residential estates and renovation projects, the kind of architects and designers who notice the difference.

2000
Radius opens at 8575 Government Street, Burnaby. Rick Wilson, President & Founder.
2011
AWMAC BC Gold — Best in Quality + Best in Service. Rosebery Estate with Lamoureux Architects. Bent-laminated curved alder rotunda, slip-matched panelling, walnut furniture.
2013–2017
Best of Houzz — five consecutive years. Design + Service categories. Influencer status across the platform with 10,000+ saves.
2018
Waterfront Anigre Penthouse — diamond-bookmatched anigre ceiling, gallery wine bar with Inuit soapstone collection.
2020
Vancouverhouse BOP Penthouse — full-house millwork inside Bjarke Ingels Group's Vancouver House tower.
2025
Indian River — The Point. Slip-matched rift white oak interior, river-rock chimney column, tongue-and-groove stair. Photography by Andrew Latreille.
2027
AWMAC Submission — Old Gravel Road, Whistler. Forty-three-sheet rift-and-smoked-oak package, currently on the bench.
