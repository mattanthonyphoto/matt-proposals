How We Work

From concept to completion. In-house, end to end.

We work from your drawings or develop fresh ideas with the design team. Industry-specific CAD software, 3D renderings with texture, full mock-ups of every project — visit the shop and see your project in full function and real proportions before we commit.

Journeymen and master joiners work in unison on the production floor. Thorough preparation for stain, lacquer, or paint, using the highest quality tooling and materials. Projects shipped and installed by in-house staff. Every quality check, every site visit, every punch list — closed by the team that built it.

The Build

How a project moves through the shop.

Five phases, all in-house. Same team from the drawing review to the punch list. We measure twice, we test the joint, we don't ship a piece until it's right.

01

Concept + Drawings

We work from your drawing set, or develop the millwork package with the design team. Industry-specific CAD, shop-side detailing, sub-trade coordination upfront.

02

Drafting + Mock-Up

3D renders to scale. Material samples on the shop floor. Full mock-ups of key millwork before we cut the final stock — visit the bench and see the project in real proportions.

03

The Bench

Twenty craftspeople on the floor. Slip-matched veneers sequenced from the same flitch. Hand-cut dovetails, bent lamination, mortise-and-tenon — joinery engineered into every piece.

04

In-House Finishing

Conversion varnish to hard-wax oil, between-coat sanded. Antique cottage to high-gloss contemporary — all finishes done under our own roof, never sub-contracted.

05

Install + Punch-List

Shipped and installed by in-house staff. Every site visit and punch-list item closed by the team that built it. We don't hand off; we finish.
