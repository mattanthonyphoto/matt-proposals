The Audit

What the data says.
Verified, not estimated.

I crawled 30 pages on lrdstudio.ca. Here’s the real picture.

What’s Strong

  • SEO scores 77/100. Valid SSL, 91ms response, 36-URL sitemap, good meta title including Whistler and Squamish locations.
  • Content scores 84/100. 20 project pages with an average of 34.8 images each. 464-word homepage. Strong design philosophy articulation.
  • Professional photography. Images from our Sunridge and other sessions. Design-intent photography that shows material choices, light, and spatial composition.
  • Houzz listing active. Important directory presence for interior designers.
  • Western Living DOTY finalist (2022). Real credential. Also featured in Build Magazine. IDIBC registered with board seat. The foundation for awards is already proven.

The Specific Gaps

  • 699 of 761 images missing alt text (91.8%). This is the single biggest issue. Every image without alt text is invisible to Google Image search, which is how homeowners discover interior designers. One-time fix with permanent impact.
  • No Google Business Profile. Can’t appear on Google Maps or in the Local Pack for “interior designer Squamish” or “interior design Whistler.”
  • No video content. Zero atmospheric walkthroughs, zero material reveals, zero design process content. For interior design, video showing how light moves through a space is the most compelling content format.
  • Awards momentum has stalled since 2022. The Western Living finalist credential proved the work qualifies. But no new submissions since then. Ashley de Boer has 5+ nominations for 2025 alone. The pipeline needs to be reactivated.
  • No blog or design journal. No content targeting “interior design Whistler” or thought leadership around materials, trends, or design process.

The Bottom Line

LRD’s portfolio is genuinely beautiful and the Western Living credential proves the work is award-calibre. The issues are discoverability (699 images Google can’t see, no local presence on Maps) and momentum (no new submissions since 2022, no video content). A creative partner fixes both while keeping the content pipeline flowing.
