SEO Audit

Strong foundation.
One critical gap.

I crawled 30 pages on lrdstudio.ca. The site is well-built with good meta data, a 36-URL sitemap, and fast response times. But one issue is undermining all that photography.

What’s Working
✓
Good Technical Foundation
Valid SSL, 91ms response, 36-URL sitemap, proper canonical tags, Open Graph. Solid Squarespace build.
✓
Strong Meta Title
"LRD Studio | Interior Design - Whistler, Squamish" — clear, branded, includes key locations.
✓
20 Project Pages
Extensive portfolio with 34.8 images per page on average. Strong visual coverage.
✓
464-Word Homepage
Good content depth. Communicates the studio’s philosophy and approach.
What Needs Fixing
✗
699 of 761 Images Missing Alt Text (91.8%)
This is the single biggest issue on the site. Nearly every image is invisible to Google. For an interior design studio where visual discovery is everything, this means Google Image search (a major source of design-intent traffic) can’t index any of your work.
✗
No Structured Data (Schema.org)
Zero JSON-LD markup. Google doesn’t know LRD is an interior design studio, where you’re based, or what services you offer. Adding LocalBusiness + InteriorDesigner schema would improve local search presence.
!
Meta Description Slightly Long (163 chars)
Google truncates at ~155 characters. Trim by 8 characters so the full description displays in search results.
!
No Blog or Journal
No content strategy driving organic traffic. Articles targeting “interior design Whistler,” “Sea-to-Sky interior designer,” or material trend pieces would capture high-intent search traffic.

SEO Bottom Line

The site’s technical foundation is solid. The critical fix is alt text on 699 images, a one-time project that permanently makes your entire portfolio visible to Google Image search. Combined with schema markup, these two fixes would significantly change how the studio appears in search results.

Local SEO Bottom Line

Google Business Profile is the #1 priority. Free, 30 minutes.
