IIOur Approach

A highly personalized approach to every project we sign on to.

Our full‑service interior design team focuses on new residential builds and legacy renovation projects, with a dash of commercial projects on the side. We define a distinctive process that helps you, the client, understand the design roadmap from the beginning to the end goal of your beautifully transformed space.

We become your ambassador, your best friend, your marriage counsellor, your advocate. Whether you come to us as a home or business owner, our goal is to cultivate your vision, layer in style and function, and arrive at a design that truly speaks to who you are.

IIIProcess

Five phases, start to finish.

Every project runs through these five phases, residential or commercial, ground‑up or renovation. Timelines vary by scope, but the shape of the work is consistent.

1

Discovery

We start with the broad scale of how you live and the goals for the project. Initial consultation, site walkthrough, and a written brief that anchors every decision that follows.

2

Schematics & concept

Space planning, the first conceptual direction, mood boards, and palette presentations. The shape of the project comes into focus.

3

Design development

Millwork and furniture design, full specifications, and construction documents. Every hinge, switch, reveal, and finish specified with intention. For projects that need them, permit drawings are produced during this phase.

4

Contract administration & site review

Regular site visits during construction, coordination with the build team, and shop drawing review. We stay close to the work to make sure every detail arrives the way we drew it.

5

Furniture procurement & install

Procurement of furniture, artwork, and decor, plus the final install and styling. The last layer that turns a finished build into a home.
