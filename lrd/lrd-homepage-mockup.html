Design for Life

A diverse and dynamic interior design studio

Design is equal parts creative talent, expertise, and execution. We recognize who you are and how you enjoy space, then use that as a foundation to create an interior environment that is uniquely yours.

West Coast modern influences every aspect of what we do. Our connection to the land allows us to source locally and understand how climate and environment are essential to functional design.

What We Do

Full-service interior design

From concept to completion, we guide every detail to create spaces that feel authentically yours.

Residential Design

Custom homes, renovations, and legacy properties. We design spaces that reflect how you live — from mountain retreats to urban sanctuaries across the Sea to Sky.

02

Commercial Design

Offices, retail, wellness studios, and hospitality. Environments that elevate brands and support the people who work within them.

Meet the Founder

Lauren Ritz

Lauren spent her youth learning fine woodwork, carpentry, and contracting from her father. Her mother was a fine artist. She initially studied Theatre & Film Set Design, but transitioned to Interior Design to work with permanent spaces.

She values collaborative leadership and mutual respect among all parties. The studio's Squamish location reflects her commitment to designing locally and engaging with the natural landscape.
