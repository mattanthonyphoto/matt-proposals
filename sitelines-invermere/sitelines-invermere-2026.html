The Story So Far

Two projects in 2025.
Four in 2026.

Jordan, last year we documented two Site Lines projects together and built a strong rhythm doing it. Now you're expanding into the BC Interior with four builds breaking ground in Invermere — two ready for June, two ready for September. This proposal shows how to capture all four at the highest standard while keeping the trip economics tight.

Invermere is a long way from Squamish. The right move is to batch — one trip in June for Colman and Schmidt, one trip in September for Slingerland and Collings. That way the travel pays for itself across multiple shoots and Site Lines gets a coordinated visual record of the entire Interior portfolio in one season.

Every project in this plan is photographed to award-submission specifications from day one. High resolution, colour calibrated, composition framed for jury review. By the end of September, Site Lines will have four fully documented Interior projects ready for the website, social, RAIC and AIBC submissions, and any publication pitches you want to chase.

How the Pricing Works

Project rates are grandfathered. My standard architect rate is $3,500 per project for new clients in 2026. Site Lines is held at $2,500 (and $1,500 for exterior-only) because we've built this together over the last year. Drone aerials and twilight exteriors are part of the base service — included on every project, no add-on charge. The travel day fee is half my standard shoot day rate, applied once per trip, covering the day each trip consumes on the road. Travel actuals (vehicle, accommodation, per diem, incidentals) are billed at cost as a pass-through, estimated at $6,300 for both trips combined, with full receipts.

How the Two Trips Run

Two trips.
Four projects. Zero wasted travel.

Both trips follow the same rhythm: drive in, shoot back-to-back days, drive home. Batching the projects into two coordinated visits is what makes the economics work for a market this far from Squamish.

June 2026
Trip 1:
Colman + Schmidt
  • Squamish to Invermere drive — gear loaded, full kit on the road
  • Day 1: Schmidt — interiors, details, design documentation
  • Day 2: Schmidt — exteriors, drone, twilight session
  • Day 3: Colman — full exterior coverage, drone, hero shots
  • Optional buffer day for weather or second twilight
  • Drive home, ingest and back up before edit begins
July 2026
Delivery:
June Trip Edits
  • Full edit and delivery within 10–15 business days of return
  • Schmidt: 20–25 final images, web + print + social formats
  • Colman: 10–15 final exterior images, web + print + social
  • Drone deliverables packaged separately for easy use
  • Cost-share outreach to builders and trades for both projects
  • Travel reconciliation with full receipts
September 2026
Trip 2:
Slingerland + Collings
  • Second Invermere trip — same kit, same rhythm
  • Day 1: Slingerland — interiors + design documentation
  • Day 2: Slingerland — exteriors, drone, twilight
  • Day 3: Collings — interiors, exteriors, drone
  • Day 4: Collings — twilight + buffer / second look
  • Hero project gets the extended shoot day if Award-Ready is selected
October 2026
Delivery:
Sept Trip + Year-End Wrap
  • Slingerland and Collings final edits delivered
  • Full Interior portfolio review across all four projects
  • Award submission shortlist — which projects, which categories
  • Publication pitch shortlist — Western Living, Azure, ArchDaily
  • Cost-share reconciliation
  • Plan for 2027 — what's next in the Interior expansion
