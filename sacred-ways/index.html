About the Retreat

Where the natural and spiritual worlds intertwine

In Hawaiian culture, the islands are recognized as sacred places where mana — the spiritual life force — flows through all living things. This retreat is your invitation to step into that current.

Rooted in Peruvian shamanism and held in the feminine, nurturing embrace of Hawai'i, Sacred Ways weaves deep ceremonial work with adventure, integration, and connection to the land.

"The islands hold the frequency of transformation. All you have to do is show up."
— Sol, Retreat Guide

The Sanctuary

A private sanctuary on Hawai'i's East coast

Perched above the ocean in Kalapana, surrounded by lush tropical gardens, walking trails, and natural spaces designed for rest and deep integration.

The ceremonial space is an open-air shala that allows the elements to be fully present throughout the work — wind, rain, sun — while still providing shelter and intimacy.
