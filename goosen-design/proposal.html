What I Do

I'm Matt Anthony — architectural and interiors photographer based in Squamish. I shoot for designers, architects, and builders across BC, with a particular focus on editorial-grade work that gets published, wins awards, and feeds a content engine year-round.

My core difference: I don't shoot rooms. I shoot the design intent — the site, the light, the materials, the moments that carry the decisions you spent six months making. The photography that lives in publications, not real estate listings.

I work with designers in two ways: per-project shoots, and a Creative Partner retainer (the model I built with Balmoral Construction, where I act as their dedicated visual partner across every project).

For Goosen Design specifically, the retainer is the right shape — you don't have one project to fix, you have a body of work to rebuild and a content engine to start.
