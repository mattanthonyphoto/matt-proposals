SEO & Site Audit

40 years of work.
3 years of marketing.

I crawled gdnielsen.com, pulled the sitemap, all 26 project pages, the source HTML, and every social channel I could find. The built work is in the top tier of NV/WV builders. The website doesn't reflect that.

What's Working
✓
Squarespace Brine Template
Solid performance baseline. Mobile responsive by default. Good foundation to rebuild on.
✓
Sitemap Healthy
89KB sitemap.xml lists all pages. No technical crawl barriers.
✓
Strong Project Photography on ~5 Projects
Marine Manor, Ocean Front Estate, Kerrisdale Zen, West Van Sanctuary, Sentinel — these have polished pro-grade exteriors and interiors. Proof the standard exists.
✓
Real Awards Behind You
Georgie wins, Ovation wins, Avid Custom Builder award (100% client recommendation), CHBA Best Home Builder BC 2005, BBB A+. Credentials are in place.
What Needs Fixing
✗
Page Title Is <title>"GD Nielsen Homes "</title>
Trailing space, no keywords. Terrible. Easy fix that's never been done.
✗
No Meta Description on Homepage
Squarespace is auto-generating a fallback. Fixable own-goal.
✗
Zero JSON-LD Schema Markup
No LocalBusiness, no Organization, no BreadcrumbList. Leaving Google rich-result eligibility on the table.
✗
10+ Projects Show Only One Exterior Photo
Casa at the Cove, Cammeray Heights, Forested Family Estate (your Georgie kitchen winner), Dramatic Shingle Style, Spectacular Stone Chimney, Rockwood, Peaceful Serenity — single hero exterior shot only. 40 to 50 percent of the portfolio is showcased with one photo each.
✗
23 of 26 Projects Have No Narrative
Only Casa at the Cove, Howe Sound Haven, and Vista Estate have any descriptive text beyond "Location: West Vancouver". No square footage anywhere on the site. No completion year. No materials or program description.
✗
Zero Photographer Credits
Not a single project page credits a photographer. Symptom of an unstable photography supply chain.
✗
Open Graph Image Is the Logo on White
Every social share — every link a client posts — looks weak. Should be a hero project image per page.
!
Half-Built Mandarin Section, No Hreflang
5 of 8 pages translated. No hreflang annotations. No Chinese projects index. International play targeting Mainland Chinese buyers — currently more SEO harm than good.
!
Orphan /home-3 Page
Indexable test page that should be deleted.

SEO Bottom Line

The technical platform is fine. The content layer is broken. 40 to 50 percent of your portfolio is shown with one exterior photo, including Forested Family Estate — your Georgie kitchen winner with no kitchen photo on its own page. That single sentence is the entire pitch.

Local SEO & Reputation

For a 40-year luxury builder doing $1.5M to $10M projects, the public reputation footprint is dramatically smaller than the deserved one.

Reviews & Reputation
✗
Houzz: 26 Projects, 0 Reviews, 0 "Best of Houzz" Badges
The single most embarrassing data point in the audit. A 40-year-old luxury builder doing $10M projects with zero Houzz reviews is, in 2026, a credibility hole any sophisticated client will notice. The page literally says "You could be the first review."
✗
Only 4 Google Reviews
5-star average, but only 4 reviews. For 40 years of work and ~1 home per year minimum, an enormous testimonial gap.
!
BBB A+ But NOT Accredited
Your awards page claims "BBB Accredited Business." The BBB record shows A+ rating but no accreditation. Worth tactfully fixing — these claims hurt credibility when verified.
Social Channels
✗
Instagram @gdnielsenhomes — Last Reel March 2023
Roughly dormant for ~3 years. For comparison, Hasler Homes (your most direct NV competitor) has 9,572 followers and 1,766 posts and just won 2026 Georgie Custom Home Builder of the Year.
✗
Two Facebook Pages, ~731 Followers Combined
Duplicate pages exist and need consolidation. Nobody is actively managing them.
✗
LinkedIn — 171 Followers, No Recent Posts
Bio says "One of BC's top high end residential construction firms." 171 followers don't see it because nothing is being posted.
✗
No YouTube, No Pinterest
For a luxury home builder, both are missed channels. Pinterest alone could drive significant referral traffic.

Local SEO Bottom Line

You have a 40-year reputation and an Avid Custom Builder award for 100% client recommendation. Public proof of that record is almost nothing: 4 Google reviews, 0 Houzz reviews, dormant social channels. Fixing this is straightforward — a structured review-request campaign tied to project completions, paired with a dormant-channel reactivation.
