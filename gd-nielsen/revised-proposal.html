Component 03

Build documentation books.
$7,000 per project.

The full story of a build, from the ground up. Every site visit produces content for both the book and your social media — two assets built simultaneously.

Documentation Visits

  • Up to 10 site visits across the build (foundation through finishing)
  • 1–2 hours per visit
  • Progress photography at key milestones — excavation, framing, envelope, mechanical, finishing, staging
  • BTS and crew content captured alongside editorial documentation

Final Portfolio Shoot

  • Full completed-project photography ($2,000 value)
  • Submission-grade hero images for awards, press, and website
  • Included in the book package — not billed separately

The Book

  • Designed and laid out as a printed documentation book
  • Chronological build story from site clearing to final walkthrough
  • Professional print coordination

The Social Media Bonus

  • Every visit produces 10–20 images that feed your content calendar
  • Two assets built at once — the book and months of social content
  • In-progress content that followers and potential clients love

How It Works

Ideal to start from the beginning of a build. Once a project is selected, we map out the key milestones and schedule visits accordingly. A typical custom home timeline is 12–18 months, so visits are roughly monthly. Each visit is $500 (up to 10), plus the final portfolio shoot at $2,000, plus the designed book — all bundled at $7,000 per project.
