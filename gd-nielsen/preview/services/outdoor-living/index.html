Coordination

Working with the landscape architect.

Outdoor projects succeed or fail on coordination. The design is one practice; the build is another. The handoff between them is ours.

We work with Sierra Landscaping, Hapa Collaborative, Living Radius, and a handful of other landscape architects across the North Shore. Each has a different drawing convention, a different sequencing preference, and a different idea of what gets handed off when. We hold the construction schedule and make sure the grading, drainage, irrigation, planting beds, and hardscape line up with the architect’s building — on the ground, not just on the drawing.

The pool excavation has to happen before the patio. The patio has to be detailed for the irrigation. The irrigation has to be in before the planting. The fence and the gate have to be in before the planting goes near them. When the trades are sequenced right, the landscape architect’s design lands the way it was drawn.
