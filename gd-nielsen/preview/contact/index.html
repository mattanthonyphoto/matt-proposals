II Before you write

A few details that help us reply well.

We don’t need a polished brief. A short note covering any of the below is plenty — whatever you have on hand. If it’s early days and you’re still thinking, that’s fine too.

  • The lot or address. Whether it’s a tear-down, a renovation, or a vacant build site.
  • Architect and drawings. Who you’re working with, or where you are in design.
  • Budget range. A band is fine. Helps us flag fit before either of us spends time.
  • Timeline. When you’d like to break ground, or when you need to be in.
  • What you’ve already discussed. With your architect, designer, or another builder.

Attach drawings or a site photo if it’s easy. PDFs welcome. If your project is confidential, just say so — we treat every inquiry that way regardless.

What happens next

From your first note to a budget framework.

Four steps, roughly four to six weeks. No obligation at any point — we’d rather both sides walk away clear than rush a match.

01 Within 2 business days Reply from Greg.

A real response — not a form acknowledgment. We’ll flag any obvious questions and propose a time to talk.

02 Week 1 · 30 minutes Initial call.

Phone or video. We talk through your lot, your drawings, the team you’re assembling, and where we might fit. Honest if we’re not the right match.

03 Week 2–3 Site visit.

If there’s mutual interest, Greg walks the lot or the existing home with you. Two hours, no cost. We surface the constructability questions a set of drawings can’t.

04 Week 4–6 Preliminary budget.

A real number framed against your drawings — not a square-foot guess. From there, you decide whether to engage us for pre-construction and a hard contract.

III Where we work

North Vancouver office. Builds across the Lower Mainland.

Our office sits on Welch Street in North Vancouver, fifteen minutes from most of the lots we build on. The majority of our work runs through West Vancouver — Altamont, Caulfeild, the British Properties, Chartwell, Cypress Park, and Dundarave — and across the North Shore.

We also build across Vancouver and into Greater Vancouver: Burnaby, Richmond, Langley, White Rock, South Surrey, and Tsawwassen. For larger custom homes anywhere in the Lower Mainland, we’ll consider the project.

Greg Nielsen is on every project — first conversation through one-year warranty walk. That’s the part that’s never delegated.
