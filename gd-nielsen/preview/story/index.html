What We Build

Custom homes, built to the architect’s drawings.

Ground-up custom homes, luxury renovations, additions, and outdoor living — built for the architects and clients who hire us. Budgets typically run from $1.5M to $10M and beyond.

We take on six to eight projects a year. Most are in West Vancouver; the rest sit between North Vancouver, Vancouver, and the wider Lower Mainland — Burnaby, Richmond, Langley, White Rock, South Surrey, and Tsawwassen. Twenty-two of the twenty-six homes in our current portfolio are West Vancouver builds.

The work is architect-driven. We follow the drawings. Greg is on every site — not as a visit, as a habit — and forty years of West Vancouver District knowledge shows up in the parts of the build that never make it into a photograph.

“We build your home as if it is our own.”

Greg Nielsen · Founder

Trade Partners & Architects

The same names, project after project.

Architects we work with often: Kallweit Graham Architecture, John Medland Architect, Craig Chevalier Designs, Living Radius, Rommel Design, and Alex Voth Design.

Interior design varies by project. Recent collaborators include Alda Pereira Design on the 2024 Bellevue residence, among others selected for the work in front of them. Landscape partners include Sierra Landscaping (Cherbourg) and Hapa Collaborative.

Our trades are the same crew, year over year. Detail-oriented collaboration is the only way the drawings make it into the wall the way they were intended.
