The Story

Forty years of craft.
One home at a time.

Since 1986, GD Nielsen Homes has been building custom homes across North and West Vancouver. We work with discerning homeowners, architects, and designers to bring exceptional residential projects to life — from waterfront estates to mountain retreats.

Craftsmanship, precision, and a passionate, experienced team. Our four-decade history is built on collaboration with the best architects and designers on the coast, attention to every material and detail, and a 100% client recommendation rate that we have earned one home at a time.

We do it right the first time. We always meet the budgets and timelines. And we never cut corners.

How We Build

A process built
around you.

Every home starts with a conversation. From the first design discussion to the final walk-through, our process is collaborative, transparent, and built around the way you actually want to live.

01

Discovery

We begin by understanding your vision, your site, your budget, and your timeline. Working alongside your architect and designer, we map out what is possible.

02

Design + Plan

We provide design and building recommendations grounded in 40 years of expertise. Material selections, construction methods, and budget alignment all happen here.

03

Build

Our experienced team executes with the craftsmanship, precision, and care that has earned us a 100% client recommendation rate over four decades.

04

Welcome Home

We deliver on time, on budget, and to the standard you expect. Every home leaves our hands ready for the family that will fill it.
