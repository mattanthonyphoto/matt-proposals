Section four · Creative direction

How a shoot runs
without you on-site.

You said you'd send a one-page brief per project with the story angle and talking points, and that I'd guide the client through it on the day. Here's the workflow that makes that work cleanly, plus the reference reels you sent and what I took from them.

  1. Brief lands 5 to 7 days before shoot. One page from your team, story angle plus 3 to 5 talking points and any must-have visual moments.
  2. I send a 30-min pre-shoot call invite to the project lead. Walk them through the talking points, set expectations, get them comfortable.
  3. On-site, I run the shoot. Lav-mic setup, multiple takes per talking point, broll captured around the dialogue. Client doesn't need media training, I guide cuts as we go.
  4. Selects sent within 5 to 7 business days. You review, mark cuts you want emphasized, I deliver final reel within 7 to 10 business days from selects approval.
  5. One revision round per deliverable, included. Additional rounds at $150/hr, scoped before work begins.
Your reference reels

I went through every reel you sent. The thread across them is the same: tight pacing, clean lav audio, founder-as-narrator anchoring the story, b-roll cut to breathing room rather than music drops. Landscape architecture, build process, and team culture all sit comfortably in this register.

My take: the Coming Soon and Project Complete cuts work best when the founder talks directly about a constraint or surprise on the project (not generic "we love what we do" energy). The Team Culture cuts read warmest when they're shot during real working moments, not staged B-roll. I'd suggest filming culture cuts during the same shoot day as Project Complete to keep continuity.

  • Six reels reviewed and noted
  • Founder-narrator anchoring across all six
  • Mid-tempo cuts, lav audio carries the pacing
  • Landscape and build-process register matches the work

Section six · Working terms

How the partnership runs.

Credits · Content tiers

Spaces Uncovered handles client-facing social posting on the client's own channels. I retain the right to display all content work on my website, journal, and case studies, with project name credited. No exclusivity restrictions on my own portfolio use.

Credits · Portfolio + Awards

Awards and portfolio shoots are credited publicly under Matt Anthony Photography across awards entries, magazine pitches, and my website. Spaces Uncovered receives a 10 percent referral fee on every awards project brought to me.

Image rights

I retain copyright across all work. The agency receives unlimited usage license for client deliverables on content shoots. Sub-licensing to publications, awards juries, or third parties routes through me for approval.

Payment

Fifty percent on shoot booking, fifty percent on delivery. Net-15 from invoice date. Quarterly retainers billed at the start of each quarter. Awards referral fees paid out within 14 days of project final delivery.

Turnaround

Photo delivery 5 to 7 business days. Reel edits 7 to 10 business days. Cinematic film 3 to 4 weeks. Portfolio and Awards shoots 4 to 6 weeks for full retouch.

Revisions

One round included per deliverable. Additional rounds at $150 per hour, scoped before work begins.

Cancellation

Forty-eight hours notice on shoot reschedules. Inside that window, fifty percent of the shoot fee applies. Weather reschedules are no charge.

Term

Six-month minimum on retainer tiers, then month-to-month with thirty days notice. Either side can exit with 30 days notice after the minimum.

Production team

Capture is delivered by me directly or by a vetted second shooter on my production team, depending on tier and availability. I own edit, QA, and final delivery on every project. Tier 1 photo sessions are second-shooter eligible. Tier 2 and 3 video work runs through me unless otherwise agreed. Portfolio and Awards shoots are always delivered by me personally.

Creative direction

Per-project briefs from your side (one-pager with story angle and talking points) drive each shoot. I run the on-site capture and guide the talent through the cuts you've outlined, no agency presence required.

Right of refusal

If a Spaces Uncovered booking conflicts with a direct-client commitment in my calendar, I reserve the right to propose alternate dates. No breach if direct work is already scheduled for the same window.
