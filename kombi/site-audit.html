Local SEO & GEO Audit

Local visibility.
Can North Shore clients find you?

This is the biggest gap. Kombi is invisible in local search results, Google Maps, and the Local Pack for North Vancouver builder searches.

Google Business Profile
✗
No Google Business Profile detected
Cannot appear in Google Maps, Local Pack, or "near me" searches. This is the single highest-impact fix available. A verified GBP with photos and reviews would immediately improve visibility for "general contractor North Vancouver" searches.
✗
No LocalBusiness schema
Even without a GBP, schema markup would tell Google your NAP (name, address, phone), service area, and business type. Currently Google has to guess.
Directory Presence
✓

✓

Strong trust signal. Excellent client reviews mentioning Brian and Ryan by name.
!

Major Canadian home services directory. Missing a citation opportunity.
!
Not found on CHBA member directory
Industry association listing would strengthen local authority and enable award submissions.
!
NAP consistency unverified
Name, address, and phone should be identical across website footer, Houzz, BBB, and any other listings.

What I'd Fix First

The action plan.
Priority order.

These are ordered by impact. The first three can be done in week one.

1

Google Business Profile

Create, verify, and optimize a GBP with photos, services, and service area. Immediate visibility in Google Maps and Local Pack for North Shore searches.

2

Schema Markup

Add LocalBusiness and HomeBuilder JSON-LD schema. Tell Google what Kombi is, where you're based, and what you do. 30-minute fix with massive SEO impact.

3

Project Portfolio

Build dedicated project pages for your best completed work. Each page is a new search entry point. Start with 3 projects and add one per month.

4

Professional Photography

Document current and upcoming projects with award-ready photography. Build the visual library that powers everything else, the portfolio, social media, awards, proposals.

5

Content Strategy

Monthly blog posts, project case studies, and social media content. Build the search authority and online presence that matches 31 years of craftsmanship.
