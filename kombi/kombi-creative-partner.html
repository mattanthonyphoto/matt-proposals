The Audit

What the data says.
Verified, not estimated.

I crawled kombihomes.com. Here's what I found across 2 pages.

What's Strong

  • SEO fundamentals score 85/100. Valid SSL, good meta title, meta description present, canonical tags, Open Graph. The technical foundation is solid.
  • Visual assets score 92/100. 12 quality images on the homepage carousel. The photography shows real craftsmanship. Almost all images have proper alt text.
  • Houzz listing active. Directory presence established for design-build in North Vancouver.
  • Fast server response (541ms). Clean, lightweight site. No performance issues.

The Specific Gaps

  • Only 2 pages on the entire site. A homepage and a contact page. 31 years of custom homes, renovations, and complex builds with zero project pages to show for it. Every project page is a new search entry point you're missing.
  • 160 words of content total. Google ranks pages based on content depth. Your competitors have thousands of words across dozens of pages. Kombi has a paragraph.
  • No project portfolio at all. The homepage carousel shows the work but there's nowhere for a potential client to explore individual projects, read about scope, or see before-and-after transformations.
  • No Google Business Profile detected. Can't appear in Google Maps or the Local Pack for "general contractor North Vancouver" searches. This is the highest-impact gap.
  • No structured data. Zero schema markup. Google doesn't know Kombi is a builder, where you're based, what services you offer, or your 31-year track record.
  • No blog, no case studies, no testimonials on site. Your BBB reviews are excellent. Ryan Heathfield gets called out by name for quality. None of this social proof lives on your own site.
  • Zero award submissions. HAVAN and Georgie awards are available for North Shore builders. Kombi's work qualifies. The photography to submit doesn't exist yet.

The Bottom Line

Kombi has strong technical SEO fundamentals and genuine craftsmanship visible even in a homepage carousel. But the site is 2 pages with 160 words. No portfolio, no project pages, no blog, no awards. The fix isn't a redesign, it's a content partner who photographs every project, builds the portfolio page by page, fixes the local SEO gaps in month one, and turns a 2-page site into a 15+ page portfolio that actually generates leads.
